## Description
XR Navigator is the college football recruiting companion built for high school athletes. Whether you're a freshman just starting out or a senior finalizing your commitment, XR Navigator gives you the tools to run your recruiting process like a pro.

FIND THE RIGHT COACHES
Search a database of 7,000+ verified college football coaches across every division — D1 FBS, D1 FCS, D2, D3, NAIA, and JUCO. Filter by division, conference, or position. Get direct contact info including email and social media.

TRACK YOUR OUTREACH
Save coaches to your personal list and track where you stand with each one. Mark coaches as contacted, waiting, or responded. Never lose track of a conversation again.

AI RECRUITING ADVISOR
Ask XR Insight anything about the recruiting process. Get personalized advice on when to reach out, what to say in emails, how to build your highlight reel, and what coaches look for at your position.

PRO FEATURES (In-App Purchase)
Upgrade to Pro for the full recruiting toolkit:
- Up to 15 AI-powered film evaluations per month
- Training video analysis with form feedback
- 8-week personalized recruiting plans
- Daily recruiting missions
- Unlimited coach views and AI messages
- Shareable recruiting cards

BUILT FOR ATHLETES
Dark theme designed for focus. Haptic feedback on every action. Offline caching so your data is always available. Your recruiting journey, in your pocket.
