## Description

XR Navigator is the recruiting companion every high school football player needs. Search 7,000+ verified college coaches across every division — from D1 FBS Power 4 programs to NAIA and JUCO — and take control of your recruiting journey.

**FIND THE RIGHT COACHES**
Search and filter coaches by school, division, position, state, and conference. Save coaches to your personal list and track every step of your outreach — from first contact to response.

**AI-POWERED RECRUITING GUIDANCE**
XR Insight, your personal AI recruiting assistant, answers questions about the recruiting process, helps you craft messages to coaches, and gives personalized advice based on your profile and goals.

**TRACK YOUR PROGRESS**
Stay motivated with daily missions, streak tracking, and XP rewards. See your recruiting pipeline at a glance — how many coaches you've saved, contacted, and heard back from.

**DISCOVER WHO'S WATCHING**
Connect your Twitter/X account to see which college coaches already follow you. Find hidden opportunities you didn't know existed.

**SHARE YOUR HIGHLIGHTS**
Generate professional recruiting graphics from your film evaluations and share them directly to social media.

**PRO FEATURES**
Upgrade to Pro for unlimited AI messages, daily recruiting missions, advanced analytics, and more.

Built by recruiters, for athletes. XR Navigator gives you the tools that used to be reserved for elite prospects — now available to every player.
